World Map Predictive Visualizer

We help you visualize country indicators through a world map with predictive capabilities. The color gradient of a given country will be calculated based on chosen weights of various indicators.

How to Use

First, click "Go to World Map" below to bring you to the "Initial Setup" page. On this page, you will select the year you would like to look at, and choose weights for each variable.

Next, click submit to view the generated world map. Your submitted values show up on the left of the screen. You can mouse over each country to see the calculated "coefficient" from your weighted variables. This number is solely for comparison within that specific year's generated map in order to identify how well a country matches your criteria. In order to view the actual values of each indicator, use the search box on the right hand side to select a country, and click submit. The indicator values will display below.
